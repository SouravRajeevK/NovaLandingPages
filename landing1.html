Frequently asked questions

Every situation is unique. These short answers cover what people ask most — tap a question to read more.

What is Nova, and who is it for?

Nova is for adults in England and Wales facing separation or divorce, or supporting someone who is. We combine technology with regulated family-law and dispute-resolution expertise to help you understand options and next steps.

Is Nova a law firm? Will I get legal advice?

Nova is not a law firm. Depending on your answers, we may point you toward mediation, DIY routes, consultants, or regulated legal advice. Where legal advice is appropriate, it comes from qualified professionals — not from an anonymous chatbot.

What does “Get started now” do?

You’ll begin a secure questionnaire (about 5–10 minutes). From that, you receive a free, personalised recommendation and a clear view of what could happen next — before you commit to any paid professional work.

What does it cost?

The technology path to your initial recommendation is free. If you choose to work with a professional, their fees apply separately. A Strategic Advice Consultation is around £299 including VAT (about one hour), with a Recommendations & Next Steps letter within two working days.

What are Nova Navigators?

Navigators help you use the platform and understand what comes next. They are not a substitute for legal advice from a regulated lawyer when you need it.
